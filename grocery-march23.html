Monday · March 23
Blanket Dumplings
▾
Ingredients
  • 1lb ground pork
  • 3 green onions, finely sliced
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil · 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • ½ tsp black pepper · ½ tsp white pepper
  • Wonton wrappers · 1–2 tbsp oil for the pan
Instructions
  1. Combine all filling ingredients and mix until fully combined.
  2. Heat a tiny bit of oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Cookie scoop or hand-roll pork into small meatballs in the pan.
  4. Lay a wonton wrapper on top of each and lightly press down.
  5. Cook 2–3 min until bottoms are golden.
  6. Add ⅓ cup water, cover, steam 5–6 min until cooked through.
  7. Remove lid, let moisture cook off.
  8. Top with gyoza sauce, chili crunch oil, sesame seeds & green onions ✨

Tuesday · March 24
Grilled Elote Steak Tacos
▾
Steak & Elote Ingredients
  • 2 ribeye steaks · Salt & pepper to taste
  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise · 2 tbsp sour cream
  • ¼ cup cilantro, chopped · Juice + zest of 1 lime
  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas
Steak & Elote Instructions
  1. Preheat grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Season ribeyes generously with salt & pepper. Let sit at room temp for 15 minutes.
  3. Grill steaks 4–5 min per side for medium-rare. Rest 5 min, then slice thinly.
  4. Grill husked corn 10–12 min, turning until slightly charred. Cool slightly, then cut kernels off the cob.
  5. In a bowl, combine mayo, sour cream, cilantro, lime juice + zest. Mix well.
  6. Warm tortillas on the grill. Assemble tacos: steak + grilled corn + sauce ✨
Homemade Flour Tortillas
Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour · 1 tsp baking powder · 1 tsp fine salt
  • ¼ cup olive oil (or avocado oil or unsalted butter) · ¾ cup warm water
Instructions
  1. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  2. Add oil and warm water. Mix with hands until dough forms and no dry bits remain.
  3. Knead on a floured surface for 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Divide into 8 pieces (burrito-size) or 10 pieces (taco-size). Roll each into a ball.
  5. Place on parchment-lined baking sheet, cover with a towel, rest at least 30 min (up to 2 hrs).
  6. Roll each ball into a 6–10 inch circle on a lightly floured surface.
  7. Cook in a dry skillet over medium-high heat, 30–45 sec per side until bubbles form and brown spots appear.
  8. Keep warm in a tortilla warmer or covered with a towel ✨

Wednesday · March 25
Honey Garlic Chicken + Fried Rice
▾
Honey Garlic Chicken
Ingredients
  • Cubed chicken · Olive oil · Kinder's "The Blend" seasoning
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce · 1 tbsp cornstarch · 1 tsp baking soda · ½ tbsp brown sugar
  • High smoke point oil · Butter · 1–2 tbsp garlic · ~1 cup soy sauce · ⅓ cup brown sugar · ½ tbsp honey
  • Optional: ginger, red pepper flakes
Instructions
  1. Combine chicken with olive oil, 1 tbsp soy sauce, cornstarch, baking soda, ½ tbsp brown sugar, and seasoning. Mix well, let sit 15–20 min.
  2. Heat pan with high smoke point oil, cook chicken until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In same pan, add butter, garlic, and optional ginger. Sauté until fragrant. Add soy sauce, brown sugar, and honey. Simmer until slightly thickened.
  4. Add chicken back in, toss until fully coated. Finish with red pepper flakes if desired ✨
Fried Rice
Ingredients
  • 2 cups cooked day-old rice · 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1–2 tbsp oil · 2–3 cloves garlic · 1 tsp ginger (optional)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables · 3–4 tbsp soy sauce · 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2–3 green onions · Salt & pepper to taste
Instructions
  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high. Cook diced chicken, season lightly, cook until golden. Remove and set aside.
  2. Push to one side, add eggs and scramble until just cooked. Set aside with chicken.
  3. Sauté garlic (and ginger if using) until fragrant. Add mixed vegetables, cook 2–3 min.
  4. Add cold day-old rice, break it apart, stir-fry until heated through and slightly crispy.
  5. Add chicken and eggs back in. Pour in soy sauce and sesame oil, toss until evenly coated.
  6. Top with green onions, taste, and adjust seasoning ✨
