Monday · March 30
Mediterranean Sheet Pan Chicken
▾
Greek Chicken Marinade
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice (about 2 lemons)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano (or 3 tbsp fresh)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme · 1 tsp salt · ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika (optional, for color)
  1. Whisk all marinade ingredients together in a bowl or shake in a jar until combined.
  2. Pour over chicken in a zip-top bag or shallow dish. Reserve a portion before adding raw chicken if you want sauce for serving.
  3. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes, up to 4 hours.
Sheet Pan — Ingredients
  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or 2 lbs bone-in thighs)
  • 1 lb baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • ½ cup pitted Kalamata olives
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for vegetables)
  • Crumbled feta · Fresh parsley or oregano · Lemon wedges
Sheet Pan — Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Arrange potatoes and onion wedges on the baking sheet. Toss with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread in a single layer.
  3. Roast vegetables for 15 minutes while chicken finishes marinating.
  4. Remove pan from oven. Push vegetables to the sides and place marinated chicken in the center.
  5. Scatter cherry tomatoes and Kalamata olives around the chicken.
  6. Roast 20–25 min for boneless (35–40 min for bone-in) until chicken reaches 165°F and vegetables are golden and tender.
  7. Let chicken rest 5 minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve topped with crumbled feta, fresh herbs, and lemon wedges ✨

Tuesday · March 31
Sweet Potato + Ground Beef Bowls
▾
Ingredients
  • Ground beef · Sweet potato · Zucchini · Broccoli
  • Garlic powder · Minced onions · Salt + pepper
  • 1½ tbsp honey · 1 tbsp mustard · 6 tbsp mayo or Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar · ⅛ tsp garlic powder · ⅛ tsp paprika · Black pepper
Veggies + Beef Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Season sweet potatoes with salt and pepper. Roast for 15 minutes.
  2. Season zucchini and broccoli with salt and pepper, then add to the sheet pan with sweet potatoes.
  3. Cook everything together for another 15–20 minutes until golden and tender.
  4. Meanwhile, cook ground beef in a pan. Season with garlic powder, minced onions, salt and pepper.
Homemade Honey Mustard
  1. Whisk together mayo (or Greek yogurt), mustard, apple cider vinegar, honey, black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Makes about ½ cup. Keeps 3–5 days in the fridge in an airtight container.
  3. Assemble bowls with veggies, beef, and honey mustard drizzled on top ✨

Wednesday · April 1
Cheeseburger Plate with Caramelized Onions + Arugula Caesar
▾
Caramelize the Onions (do this first!)
  • 1 large yellow onion, thinly sliced · 1 tbsp butter · Pinch of salt
  • Optional: splash of balsamic vinegar
  1. Melt butter in a pan over medium-low heat.
  2. Add sliced onions + pinch of salt. Cook slowly 20–30 min, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once golden and soft, add a splash of balsamic if desired ✨ This is what makes the dish.
Make the Burgers
  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20) · Salt + pepper · 2–4 slices cheddar · 1 tbsp butter or oil
  1. Form beef into patties — don't overwork it.
  2. Season both sides with salt + pepper.
  3. Cook in a hot pan ~3–4 min per side.
  4. Add cheese at the end and let it melt.
Arugula Caesar
  • 2–3 cups arugula · ¼ cup grated parmesan
  • ¼ cup mayo · 1 tbsp lemon juice · 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove, minced · 2 tbsp grated parmesan · Salt + pepper · Splash of water to thin
  1. Mix all dressing ingredients together until smooth. Taste + adjust (more lemon = brighter, more parm = richer).
  2. Combine arugula + parmesan, toss with dressing right before serving.
  3. Plate burger patties, top with a generous pile of caramelized onions, add arugula caesar on the side ✨
