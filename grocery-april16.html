Monday · April 6
Honey Lime Chicken Rice Bowls
▾
Chicken Marinade
  • 10–12 boneless chicken thighs
  • ⅓ cup olive oil · 1 tbsp paprika · 1 tbsp salt · 1 tbsp onion powder · 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cumin · ½ tsp red chili flakes · 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 tbsp honey · freshly cracked black pepper · 2 limes, juiced & zested
Bowl Ingredients
  • 2 cups cooked white rice (cooked in broth for extra flavor)
  • ½ cup pickled onions · 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 4 mini cucumbers, sliced · 2 ripe avocados, sliced
Honey-Lime Dressing
  • ½ cup olive oil · 1 tbsp red wine vinegar · 1 tbsp Dijon mustard · 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp salt · ½ tsp red chili flakes · freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1 garlic clove, grated · 1 lime, juiced & zested · 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
Instructions
  1. Whisk together all marinade ingredients. Add chicken and coat well. Marinate at least 30 minutes, up to 4 hours.
  2. Rinse rice and cook in broth for extra flavor. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, cook until fluffy. Rest covered 5 minutes.
  3. Grill marinated chicken on each side until golden and cooked through. Brush extra marinade on as you grill. Let rest, then slice.
  4. Whisk together all dressing ingredients in a small bowl or jar until well combined.
  5. Assemble bowls: warm rice base, sliced chicken, cucumbers, avocado, cilantro, and pickled onions. Drizzle generously with honey-lime dressing ✨

Tuesday · April 7
Greek Meatball Bowls
▾
Meatball Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground beef · 3 garlic cloves, finely minced · 2 medium onions, finely minced
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs · 1 tsp baking soda · 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup fresh herbs (parsley, mint, coriander), finely chopped
  • ½ tsp salt · black pepper to taste
  1. Mix all meatball ingredients together in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours — the baking soda makes them extra tender.
  2. Roll into small meatballs.
  3. To bake: 425°F for 30–40 minutes until browned and cooked through. To pan-fry: cook in a skillet with olive oil 10–15 min, turning occasionally.
Roasted Potatoes
  • 6 medium potatoes, cut into wedges · 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp each: salt, cumin, paprika, dried oregano · black pepper to taste
  1. Toss potato wedges with olive oil and all spices. Spread on a baking sheet.
  2. Roast at 425°F for 40–60 minutes, flipping halfway, until crispy and golden.
Tzatziki Sauce
  • 1 cup thick Greek yogurt · ½ cucumber, grated and squeezed dry
  • 1 garlic clove, minced · 1 tbsp lemon juice · ⅓ cup fresh dill or mint, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil · salt to taste
  1. Combine all tzatziki ingredients, stir well, and chill until ready to serve.
Build the Bowl
  • 2 cups crisp lettuce · 2 tomatoes, chopped · 2 cucumbers, chopped
  • ½ red onion, chopped · ½ cup Kalamata olives
  • 2 tbsp olive oil · 1 tbsp lemon juice · salt + pepper
  1. Start with a layer of crisp lettuce. Add Greek salad (tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives dressed with olive oil, lemon, salt + pepper).
  2. Add a handful of roasted potato wedges and top with meatballs.
  3. Finish with a big spoonful of tzatziki and extra fresh herbs ✨

Wednesday · April 8
Turkey Burgers
▾
Ingredients
  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise · 1 tbsp Dijon mustard · 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced · 1 tsp kosher salt · ½ tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Optional Toppings
  • Cheddar cheese · sliced tomato · sliced onion · caramelized onion
  • Pickles · lettuce · ketchup, mustard, and mayo
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, combine turkey, mayo, Dijon, Worcestershire, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix with your hands until combined.
  2. Divide evenly and form 4 large patties — don't overwork it.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook patties ~5 min per side until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Add cheese at the end if using and let melt. Serve plain or load up with your favorite toppings ✨
